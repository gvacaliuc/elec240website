Pin Connections and the Interface Module

The path from the instruments to the breadboard is provided by the Interface Module. This is a printed circuit board with a variety of different connectors and two short, single-row socket strips.

These are like the socket strips on the breadboard (five points connected together) except that instead of being connected just to themselves, the pins are also connected to the other connectors on the board.
