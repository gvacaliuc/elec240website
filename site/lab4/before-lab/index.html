ELEC 240 Lab

Before Lab

Up to now we've been building fairly simple circuits, measuring them, and taking them apart. This week we will begin building simple subsystems, some of which we will want to use in subsequent labs. Although as subsystems they are simple, as circuits they are quite a bit more complex than those we've built so far, and wiring them will require a bit more thought on how the components should be laid out and connected on the breadboard. You should look over the section on organizing your breadboard and develop a plan of attack for this week's session.
